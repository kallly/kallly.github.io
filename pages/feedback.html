Feedback

Tower Defense Simulator Mapper is built and maintained by a member of the community, in whatever spare time is available. If something feels clunky, missing, or just plain broken while you're using it, that's exactly the kind of thing worth hearing about.

There's no form to fill out — just send an email.

Email tds-mapper@proton.me →

What counts as feedback

  • A bug — something that doesn't work the way it should.
  • A missing tower, map, or piece of data that should be there.
  • Something confusing or clunky in the interface.
  • A feature you wish the mapper had.
  • A broken link, typo, or anything else that looks off.

If in doubt, send it anyway — a short "this felt weird" email is still useful.

What helps the most

  • What you were trying to do when you noticed it.
  • What happened, versus what you expected to happen.
  • A screenshot, if it's visual.
  • Your browser and device, if it looks like a display issue.

None of this is required — even a one-line email is welcome — but the more context you give, the faster it can actually get fixed.

Every email is read, but replies and fixes aren't guaranteed on any particular timeline — this is a side project, not a support desk. Thanks for taking the time to help make the mapper better.
