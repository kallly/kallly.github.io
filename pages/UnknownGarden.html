Unknown Garden

Developer(s)Your_Adversary
DifficultyNormal
LengthExtremely Long (01:19.63)
Game modesHardcore-exclusive

Description

Unknown Garden takes place at night on a small floating island shaped like a garden sanctuary. A few void creatures rest on benches among trees, potted plants, flowers and vines, and a Two X statue stands at the center of the island. One bench sits right at the edge of a cliff, overlooking a purple river far below.

Design & Path

The path runs from a purple portal to an orange one, moving across the stone ground that winds through the garden. Being an "extremely long" map, it gives towers with strong sustained damage plenty of time to work, but also puts pressure on economy management over the course of a run.

Strategy

  • Several spots on this map are particularly strong for the Ace Pilot, thanks to open sightlines over large sections of the path.
  • The map has a lot of cliffs, which makes cliff-only towers such as the Ranger and Mortar especially valuable here.
  • Because the map is large, high-range cliff towers can cover a big portion of the path from a single spot, reducing how many towers you need overall.
