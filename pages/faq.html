Frequently Asked Questions

Below are the most common questions about Tower Defense Simulator Mapper. If you cannot find the answer you are looking for, please visit the Contact page.

What is Tower Defense Simulator Mapper?

Tower Defense Simulator Mapper is a free online tool that helps players plan their tower placements before playing Tower Defense Simulator on Roblox.

Is this website free?

Yes. The mapper is completely free to use and does not require any registration.

Do I need a Roblox account?

No. You can use the mapper without logging into Roblox.

Do you save my strategies?

No. Your work stays inside your browser unless you choose to save or export it using available features.

Is this an official Roblox website?

No. This is an independent fan-made project created for the Tower Defense Simulator community.

Can I use the mapper on mobile?

Yes. The website is designed to work on modern desktop and mobile browsers. However, a larger screen provides a better editing experience.

Will new maps be added?

Yes. Whenever possible, the mapper is updated to include new maps and support changes introduced in Tower Defense Simulator.

I found a bug. What should I do?

Please report bugs through our Contact page or open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.

Can I suggest new features?

Absolutely. Community feedback helps improve the mapper. Suggestions for new features, interface improvements and additional tools are always welcome.

Why are advertisements displayed?

Advertisements help cover the costs of maintaining the website and allow the tool to remain free for everyone.
