Wretched Front

Developer(s)Your_Adversary
DifficultyEasy
LengthLong (01:14.62)
Game modesHardcore, Voidcore

Description

Wretched Front is set deep in the Nil Zone, on a small island surrounded by a purple liquid moat. Across the moat lie several cliffs. The island itself is littered with towers linked by chains, abandoned swords and banners, and a few destroyed Siege Engines. This map is only available in the Hardcore and Voidcore rotations.

Design & Path

The path begins at a large wall decorated with eyes and a gate shaped like a mouth, then winds across the island around its obstacles before ending at a small entrance reached via a bridge.

Strategy

  • The many u-turns found here and in the central part of the map are ideal tower placement spots, since they offer excellent coverage of the path from a single position.
  • Being an Easy-difficulty map, it's a good place to test new tower combinations before taking them into harder Hardcore or Voidcore runs.
