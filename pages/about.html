About Tower Defense Simulator Mapper

Tower Defense Simulator Mapper is a free browser-based tool created to help players plan, visualize and share their strategies before entering a game. Instead of experimenting directly in-game, players can place towers on a map, test different layouts and prepare optimized defenses.

The project was created by a member of the Tower Defense Simulator community who wanted an easy way to sketch strategies without installing any software or creating an account.

Everything runs directly inside your browser. No registration is required and the tool is designed to be fast, simple and accessible from any device.

Features

  • Interactive map editor
  • Drag and place towers on maps
  • Simple strategy planning
  • Works directly in your browser
  • No installation required
  • Free to use

Our Goal

The goal of this project is to provide useful tools for the Tower Defense Simulator community. We continuously improve the mapper by fixing bugs, adding new maps and supporting new game content whenever possible.

Privacy

The mapper itself does not require an account and does not store your personal information on our servers.

If advertising services such as Google AdSense are used, third-party providers may use cookies to display relevant advertisements. Please read our Privacy Policy for more information.
