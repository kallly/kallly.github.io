Help & Controls

Placing & editing

  • Pick a tower in the left sidebar, then click on the map to place it.
  • Click a placed tower to select it — its range is shown automatically.
  • Press Delete or use the 🗑️ button to remove the selected tower.
  • Right-click (or tap elsewhere) to cancel a placement in progress.

View controls

  • Scroll the mouse wheel, or pinch with two fingers on touch, to zoom.
  • Pan with arrow keys or WASD/ZQSD, or drag with one finger on touch.
  • ◎ resets the map position. 🏹 💬 L toggle ranges, names and levels.
  • 👥 filters which players' towers are shown in full color.

Zones

  • Pick a color, then click 🖌️ to start drawing a zone.
  • Click on the map to add points; click the first point again (or press Enter) to close the shape.
  • Right-click or Esc cancels the zone being drawn.
  • Click a finished zone to select it, then Delete or 🗑️ to remove it.

Text labels

  • Click 🔤, then click on the map to place a text label (20 characters max).
  • Right-click or Esc cancels the text placement.
  • Click a placed label to select it, then Delete or 🗑️ to remove it.

Enemy path (admin)

  • Click 🧭 to start tracing the enemy path.
  • Click on the map to add points; right-click or press Enter to finish (2 points minimum).
  • Esc cancels the path being traced.
  • Click a finished path to select it, then Delete to remove it.
  • The path is not saved/shared with the rest of the map — use "View path JSON" to copy it out.

Collaboration

  • Create a session to get a room code, then share it so others can join and edit the same map live.

Keyboard shortcuts

  • Ctrl+Z — undo last action
  • Ctrl+S — save to the JSON textarea
  • Ctrl+L — load from the JSON textarea

Save, share & export

  • "Share the map" copies a link that reloads your exact plan (and current player filter) for anyone who opens it.
  • Save/Load JSON lets you copy your plan as text to store it yourself.
  • 🖼️ downloads the full map as a PNG image.
  • Zones and text labels are saved and shared right along with your towers.
